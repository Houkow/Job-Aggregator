# Product - Job Aggregator

## Product Vision

The platform is a job and internship aggregator designed to help anyone looking for a job, internship, or apprenticeship find relevant opportunities quickly and efficiently.

It centralizes offers from WeLoveDevs into a single platform and improves the search experience through filtering and structured navigation.

---

## Target Users

This platform is designed for:

- Job seekers
- Internship applicants
- Apprenticeship (alternance) candidates
- Junior developers entering the job market

👉 In short: anyone actively looking for professional opportunities.

---

## Core Problem

Users face several issues when searching for jobs:

- fragmented job sources across multiple platforms
- too many irrelevant offers
- lack of structured and efficient filtering
- time-consuming search process

---

## Solution

We provide a centralized job aggregator that:

- collects job offers from WeLoveDevs
- standardizes and structures job data
- offers advanced filtering (location, contract type, skills, salary)
- allows users to save and apply to offers
- provides recruiter posting capabilities

---

## Key Features

### 1. Authentication
- user registration and login
- role-based access (user / admin / recruiter)

### 2. Job Explorer
- browse all job offers
- filter and refine search results
- view detailed offer pages

### 3. Saved Offers
- save interesting job listings
- access them later easily

### 4. Applications
- track application status

### 5. Recruiter Features
- create and manage job offers
- view posted listings

### 6. Admin Panel
- basic moderation and system management

---

## Value Proposition

- faster and simpler job discovery
- centralized access to multiple job opportunities
- improved search experience compared to raw job boards
- better organization of saved offers

---

## Product Principle

“Helping every candidate find the right opportunity faster”

---

## Success Metrics

- number of saved offers per user
- number of applications submitted
- usage of search and filters
- engagement on job explorer page
